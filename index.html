More About Me

I am from Cameroon, and I study computer science at the University at Buffalo. I'm passionate about technology, sports, and building tech that makes a difference. Throughout my life, I’ve consistently embraced challenges that have shaped me into an assiduous and fearless individual. As a kid in Cameroon, I went from studying in French to learning just in English in high school. This was a difficult change that tested my capacity to adapt. This led to an even bolder step, leaving my home country alone as a teenager to pursue my tech ambitions here in the USA.

I am what can be described as a tech jack of all trades. I never shied of expressing my creativity through tech. I ran YouTube channels with video andaudio editing, wrote NBA blogs for a French media source when I was 14, and played around with game development using Scratch, Unity, and Blender. My passion for basketball even led me into data analytics long before I studied it formally. Now, as a Computer Science major at the University at Buffalo, I continue to grow my skills through classes and clubs like IBM Z, NSBE, and the Data Analytics Club.

When I'm not coding or advocating, you can find me playing Basketball, Soccer or Tennis. I catch NBA games every night and barcelona football games on weekends. I also love, watching animes and having game nights with my friends!
